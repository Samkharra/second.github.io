Red Light,Green Light

First Level Game

In Squid Game, the leader is a giant doll that lets players move. While she is chanting, players are allowed to run towards her. When she stops and turns, whoever moves is shot to death.

Sugar Honeycombs

Second Level Game

a sugar honeycomb with one of four different shapes modelled into it, the players have to carve out the shapes without breaking their design – if the shape doesn’t come out whole, the player is killed.

Tug of War

Third Level Game

the tug of war challenges each group of players into using all their strength and the best of their skills to make the other group fall to their death.

Marbles

Fourth Level Game

The goal is to win every marble your opponent has, but, as they quickly figure out, that between themselves and the person they chose to pair up with , one of them will die.

Mirror Bridge

Fifth Level Game

Players have to jump their way through a bridge that has two glass squares side by side. the square beside it is fragile and whoever jumps on it falls to their death.

Squid Game

Final Level Game

The game is played by drawing a square, a triangle and two circles on the ground .and has two teams trying to invade each other’s space. The tricky part is: you can’t touch any lines and must hop on one foot on most areas.
